# Unscroll - Competitive Research

## App Overview
- **Name:** Unscroll
- **Type:** Productivity/Focus Timer iOS App
- **Core Feature:** Gamified focus timer with challenges, streaks, gems, XP, levels
- **Target:** Students, remote workers, productivity enthusiasts

---

## Market Analysis

### Market Size
- Productivity app market: $50B+ (2026)
- Focus/timer apps: Growing segment within productivity
- Gamification in apps: Strong trend, especially with Gen Z

### Target Market
- **Primary:** Students (16-25) needing study focus
- **Secondary:** Remote workers, freelancers
- **Tertiary:** Anyone trying to build discipline/habits

---

## Competitors

### 1. Forest (Top Competitor)
**Strengths:**
- Established brand (10M+ downloads)
- Strong gamification - virtual tree planting
- Simple, elegant UI
- Cross-platform

**Weaknesses:**
- Paid ($4.99/month for full features)
- No social challenges
- Limited customization
- No AI integration

**Pricing:** Freemium, $4.99/month pro

---

### 2. Todoist
**Strengths:**
- Best-in-class task management
- Robust project organization
- Strong integrations

**Weaknesses:**
- No focus timer
- Not gamified
- Too complex for simple needs

**Pricing:** Freemium, $5/month premium

---

### 3. TickTick
**Strengths:**
- Built-in Pomodoro timer
- Task + calendar combo
- Habit tracking

**Weaknesses:**
- dated UI
- Gamification is secondary
- Cluttered experience

**Pricing:** Freemium, $3/month pro

---

### 4. Forest
**Strengths:**
- Social features (plant with friends)
- Real-world impact (plant real trees)

**Weaknesses:**
- Limited focus analytics
- No customization of sessions

## Differentiation Strategy

### Unscroll's Edge
1. **Deep Gamification** - XP, levels, gems, hearts - RPG-style progression
2. **Challenge System** - Daily/weekly challenges with rewards
3. **AI-Powered Insights** - Not just tracking, but AI analysis
4. **Mobile-First** - Optimized for iOS, not cross-platform compromise

### Target Segments
1. **The Gamer** - Wants rewards, progression, achievements
2. **The Achiever** - Likes streaks, completion, stats
3. **The Social** - Wants challenges with friends (future)

### Go-to-Market
- TikTok/Instagram Reels - Show gamification
- Student discounts
- Free tier with enough to be addictive
- Apple Search Ads targeting "focus app", "study app"

---

## SWOT Analysis

### Strengths
- Deep gamification (unique in market)
- iOS-optimized, modern SwiftUI
- Supabase backend for cloud sync
- AI insights feature

### Weaknesses
- New, unknown brand
- No cross-platform (Android)
- No social features yet

### Opportunities
- TikTok viral potential
- Student market
- Gamification trend growing

### Threats
- Forest is entrenched
- Big tech (Apple, Google) could enter
- Free alternatives

---

## Monetization
- **Free Tier:** Basic timer, limited gems, ads
- **Pro ($4.99/month):** Unlimited timer, no ads, AI insights, unlimited challenges
- **One-time ($14.99):** Lifetime pro
